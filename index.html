Freelancing

Whether for a business or just for personal use, having an online presence is crucial. It widens your audience and makes your products more accessible. Together we can create something that showcases your products and reaches your audiences.

Process

Consultation. It all starts with a chat. At thisstage, you tell me about your site and how you want it to be. The most important aspects and your budget.

Proposal. After I draw up a contract, which outlines what was discussed in the chat.

Development. I build your site. Throughout this I communicate with you weekly, updating on any additions and changes.

Delivery. I transfer all files into your possession. With a unique domain name.
